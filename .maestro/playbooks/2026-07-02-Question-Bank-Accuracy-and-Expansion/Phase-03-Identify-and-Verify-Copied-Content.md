**Urgent, user-prioritized.** Phase 02's cross-reference task found that a subset of `src/data/questions.ts` — tagged with dated `source` fields like `'2026-04-18 PP3'` — are verbatim or near-verbatim copies of real UHR exam questions, confirmed by diffing 5 sample items (`xyz-001`, `kva-001`, `nog-001`, `dtk-001`, `xyz-017`/`xyz-018`) against the actual exam PDFs. The aggregate count (103: 40 XYZ, 28 KVA, 24 NOG, 11 DTK) came from counting `source` fields matching the dated-exam pattern, not from individually verifying all 103. This phase closes that gap: produce a complete, individually-verified manifest before Phases 04/05 replace anything. This phase does **not** modify `src/data/questions.ts` — identification and verification only. Phase 04/05 do the actual replacement.
